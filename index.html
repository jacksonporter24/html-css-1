JACKSON PORTER

ABOUT JACKSON

Jackson Porter is a fantasy, science-fiction, and mystery author who lives in Utah. He has a B.S. in English with an emphasis in Creative Writing from Utah Valley University. While he isn't writing or reading, he enjoys movies, video games, puzzles, games, and occasionally venturing outdoors. He considers his faith, his wife, and his gluten-free diet the most interesting things about himself. Jackson is represented by Terri Baranowski of Gateway Literary Agency.
